Build a "Practice Areas" section for a law firm website with a horizontal scrolling "Case File Strip" design.
Overall Section:

Full-width section with a light off-white/cream background (#FAFAF7 or similar)
Top: small uppercase tracking label "OUR EXPERTISE" in gold (#C6A962), followed by a large serif heading "Areas of Practice" in dark navy (#1A1F4E)
Below the heading, a horizontally scrollable strip of case file folder tabs

The Case File Strip:

Horizontal scroll container (CSS overflow-x: auto, hide scrollbar or style it minimally)
Contains 8 practice area "folders" in a row, each styled like a physical manila/cream case file tab
Each folder tab is roughly 280px wide, with a slight paper/folder texture feel (subtle off-white or warm beige background, maybe a very faint drop shadow or border to suggest a physical folder edge)
Top of each tab has a small gold (#C6A962) foil-style number: 01, 02, 03… etc
Below the number, the practice area name in bold navy (#1A1F4E) serif font
Below that, a single subtle one-liner description in smaller gray text
On hover, the tab lifts slightly (translateY -4px) with a soft shadow increase, and the gold number gets brighter

Click/Expand Behavior:

When a folder tab is clicked, it "opens" — a panel expands DOWNWARD below the entire strip (not inside the tab itself), sliding open smoothly (300ms ease)
The expanded panel has a dark navy (#1A1F4E) background with:

The practice area name in large white serif text
A 2-3 sentence description in light gray
A gold "Enquire About This Area →" CTA link
A subtle gold top border or accent line connecting it to the selected tab


Only one folder can be open at a time — clicking another closes the current and opens the new one
Clicking the same tab again closes the panel

The 8 Practice Areas:

Corporate & Commercial — Structuring complex transactions, joint ventures, and commercial agreements for businesses operating across borders and sectors.
Tax & Compliance — Strategic tax planning, regulatory compliance, and advisory services to keep businesses aligned with evolving legal frameworks.
Mergers & Acquisitions — End-to-end counsel on acquisitions, disposals, restructurings, and due diligence for domestic and cross-border deals.
Startups & Venture Capital — Legal frameworks for fundraising rounds, equity structuring, founder agreements, and scaling ventures.
Intellectual Property & Technology — Protecting trademarks, patents, copyrights, trade secrets, and advising on technology licensing and data regulations.
Real Estate & Property Law — Advising on acquisitions, leasing, development projects, title disputes, and property portfolio management.
Employment & Labor Law — Navigating workforce regulations, employment contracts, terminations, and workplace dispute resolution.
Litigation & Dispute Resolution — Representing clients in civil and commercial disputes, arbitration, and enforcement proceedings across jurisdictions.

Scroll Indicators:

Left and right arrow buttons (small, circular, navy with gold arrow icon) at either end of the strip for desktop users
On mobile, the strip is swipeable with momentum scroll

Tech:

React component (or plain HTML/CSS/JS — your choice)
Use smooth scroll-behavior for the arrow buttons
Google Fonts: use "Playfair Display" for serif headings, "Inter" or "DM Sans" for body text
No external component libraries needed — keep it vanilla
Make it fully responsive: on mobile (<768px), the tabs shrink slightly and the expanded panel stacks cleanly

Vibe: Premium, tactile, understated. Think high-end stationery meets legal tradition. No flashy gradients or illustrations — just clean typography, gold accents, and paper-like warmth.
