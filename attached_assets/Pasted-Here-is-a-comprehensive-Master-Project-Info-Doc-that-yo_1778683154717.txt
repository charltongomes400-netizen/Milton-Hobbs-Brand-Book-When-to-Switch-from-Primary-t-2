Project Overview: Milton Hobbs Website Build
1. Core Identity

Client Name: Milton Hobbs

Industry: Boutique Corporate Law Firm

Tagline: "Reason. Rigor. Resolution."

Target Audience: Premium corporate clients, cross-border businesses (UAE ↔ France).

Brand Vibe: Global authority meets boutique exclusivity. Formal, modern, and highly sophisticated.

Design Inspiration: Think McKinsey & Company or Hogan Lovells. Heavy focus on thought leadership and clean, structural authority.

Strict Visual Ban: Do NOT use any cliché legal imagery under any circumstances. No gavels, no scales of justice, no pillars, and no law books.

2. Tech Stack Requirements

Framework: Next.js (React)

Styling: Tailwind CSS

Animations: Framer Motion (and Three.js for 3D backgrounds if applicable)

Localization: i18n support for English and French

3. Brand Guidelines & Color Palette

Primary Background: Crisp White (#FFFFFF). The UI must be white-dominant to avoid feeling flat or heavy.

Typography & Primary Accents: Deep Blue (#001489) and Black.

Action Accent: Yellow (Use for key highlights, buttons, or hover states to provide high contrast against the blue).

Typography Style: Clean, geometric sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Roobert, or similar).

Shapes: The brand relies on "Rigor." Use sharp, clean lines and square/geometric elements. Avoid heavily rounded corners.

4. Animation & Interaction Rules

Backgrounds: Use subtle, slow-moving 3D geometric grids or rotating glass-like square structures.

Layouts: Heavily utilize asymmetrical "Bento Box" (masonry square) grids for practice areas and content.

Micro-interactions: Implement square border-tracing animations. On hover, a crisp accent-colored line should animate around the square perimeter of cards or buttons.

5. Localization (Bilingual Requirements)

The site must fully support English (EN) and French (FR).

Include a functional EN/FR language toggle in the global header.

Structural Rule: All text containers and UI elements must be built with 20% extra padding/breathing room to naturally accommodate French text expansion without breaking the layout.

6. Sitemap & Architecture

Header Navigation: Home, Our Firm, Our Expertise, Publications & Insights, Careers, Contact.

"Our Expertise" Mega-Menu: Must contain these 10 items: Corporate & Commercial, Real Estate & Property, Litigation & Dispute Resolution, Arbitration & Mediation, Employment & Labour, Banking & Finance, Tax, Intellectual Property, Technology / Startups, Immigration.

Hidden Architecture (Critical): Build the URL routes for /our-people and /our-people/amir-fardoun. These must exist in the code but remain completely unlinked and hidden from the live UI navigation.

Footer: Must include two location columns (Dubai & Paris), contact info (contact@miltonhobbs.com), a Cookie Consent banner, and standard legal links (Privacy Policy, Cookies, Legal Disclaimers).

7. Key Page Elements (Home Page)

Hero: Geometric 3D background, tagline, and two buttons (Book a consultation / Contact us).

Differentiators Grid: 4 points (Boutique model, Trilingual fluency, Cross-border structuring, European savoir-faire).

Thought Leadership (Instead of Case Studies): A section featuring 4 custom law articles (e.g., "Navigating Cross-Border Compliance in the Gulf").
